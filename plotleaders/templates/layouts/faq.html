What is this?
On this website, you will learn more about the way people view leaders: who comes to their mind? Which qualities do they see in those leaders? Who is seen as the most authoritarian leader? The most aspirational? The least benevolent? Use our app to view the results!
How does it work?
You can use our app to generate perceptual maps of leaders:
  1. Pick one variable to view on the horizontal axis.
  2. Pick a second one to view on the vertical axis.
  3. Optionally, pick a third one to color-code the leaders.
  4. Finally, you can decide on the types of leaders you want to see: politicians, celebrities...
You can then zoom by drawing a rectangle on the portion of the graph you want to have a closer look at, and double-click to reset the zoom level.
Show me an example!
The leaders in the left part (right part) of the graph are seen as the least (most) authoritarian.

The leaders in the lower part (upper part) of the graph are seen as the least (most) competent.

The leaders represented by blue dots (red dots) are seen as the most (least) trustworthy.
What about the data?
We have collected the data presented here in two waves:
  1. First, we have asked one group of respondents to name people and characters who they view as leaders.
  2. We then asked a second group of respondents to rate those leaders on a certain number of psychological traits: authority, benevolence, warmth, competence, etc...
So far, more 2500 persons have helped us rate those leaders, thank you to each of them! If you want to help us as well, you can do the survey now!
